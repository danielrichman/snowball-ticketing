Entertainments

This year the Snowball, which in the past has played host to bands including Mumford and Sons and The Who, presents acts including the Irish folk-pop duo Hudson Taylor and the UK's no. one jive and swing band, The Jive Aces.

It will also feature performances from up-and-coming pop quintet Amber Run, who have just been signed to Sony, and the CU show choir.

Food

'Le Chat Noir' offers a feast for all the senses, and taste is not forgotten! From exotic specialities to satisfying comfort food, we have a delicacy to suit every palate. Perhaps we can even tempt you to indulge in some surprising dishes you might not be expecting...

Drink

From hot drinks in the queue to beer that is being specially brewed for the Snowball, this year's drinks will be exceptional! Enjoy delicious themed cocktails or chill out with a steaming cup of liqueur coffee in the French Café. After all, we do occasionally need to take a little break...
