Working at the Snowball

Thank you for your interest in working at Selwyn Snowball 2013! This is the only winter ball in Cambridge this year and we're really excited about working with you to make it amazing. Absolutely no experience is required, we will give you all the training necessary.

Please submit the online application form no later than 4th November and we will get back to you as soon as possible. If you have any further questions or trouble filling out the form, please feel free to email Peggy and Fiona at personnel@selwynsnowball.co.uk.

Roles Required:

  • Food & Drink
  • General
  • Security

Shifts Available:

  • 1st shift: work 8pm - 12am in return for free entry to the 2nd half of the ball
  • 2nd shift: work 12am - 4am, paid
